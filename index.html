Keratine

Treatments that involve applying a keratin-based formula to the hair, which is then sealed in using heat. The treatment can help reduce frizz, enhance shine, and make hair more manageable. Results can last several weeks to months, depending on the treatment and hair type.

Balayage

Hair coloring technique that creates a natural, sun-kissed look by hand-painting highlights onto the hair. The term "balayage" comes from the French word "balayer," which means "to sweep." This technique allows for a more blended and softer appearance compared to traditional highlighting methods.

Extensions

Hair extensions are a popular beauty accessory used to add length, volume, and fullness to natural hair. They come in various forms, including clip-in, tape-in, sew-in, fusion, micro-link, and halo extensions, each suited to different needs and lifestyles. Clip-in and halo extensions are ideal for temporary use, offering easy application and removal without damaging natural hair.

Straightning

Hair straightening is a styling technique used to flatten and smoothen hair, giving it a sleek and polished appearance. It can be achieved through various methods, both temporary and permanent. Temporary hair straightening involves the use of flat irons, blow dryers, or hot brushes, which straighten the hair by applying heat
